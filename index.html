What Is Kingdom Health?

Kingdom Health is a biblical wellness program created to help believers steward their bodies according to God's design, not modern cultural pressure.

The program includes:

  • Simple, repetitive whole-food meals
  • Progressive movement and strengthening
  • Rest and stress reduction
  • Emotional healing guided by Scripture
  • Prayer, inner healing, and deliverance (when needed and desired)

"Do you not know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit?"
1 Corinthians 6:19

Why Simplicity?

The human body was created for rhythm, consistency, and order.

Simple eating and stable routines:

  • Support digestion
  • Reduce stress and overwhelm
  • Restore hunger and fullness cues
  • Bring peace to the body

"Give us this day our daily bread."
Matthew 6:11
